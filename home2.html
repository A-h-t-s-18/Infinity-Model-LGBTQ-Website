United Nations


The United Nations promotes the rights of lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, intersex, and queer (LGBTIQ+) people as fundamental human rights. The commitment is rooted in the Universal Declaration of Human Rights and the belief that dignity and equality belong to all.
